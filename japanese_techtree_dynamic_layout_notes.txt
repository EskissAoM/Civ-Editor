Japanese dynamic right-side TechTree layout

First pass added after the Greek/Egyptian/Norse/Atlantean/Chinese systems.
Rules followed:
- Building/lane columns are reserved globally across ages.
- Each building lane is packed before the next building lane begins.
- Parent/child lines are kept in the same column where possible.
- Units with direct child techs use row 0 and their child uses row 1 where possible.
- Direct childless units prefer row 1 when the parent building is introduced in that age, unless the column also needs a childless tech.
- ArchaicAge keeps the existing row-1 preference for non-root nodes.
- WallConnector upgrade line can share the WallConnector column.
- Omniscience can share the Temple column.
- Generic bonus rules are applied: Gaia economic upgrades one age earlier, Oranos SkyPassage after Temple, Fuxi Nezha under Temple for non-Chinese, Shennong Farm in Archaic for non-Egyptian, and Thor DwarvenArmory compact layout.
- For Japanese, Nezha uses Temple, not ImperialAcademy.
